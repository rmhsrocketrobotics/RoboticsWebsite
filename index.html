RICHARD MONTGOMERY HIGH SCHOOL ROBOTICS TEAM

Rocket Robotics, FTC Team 22565, is an open team of students at Richard Montgomery High School that anyone can join. We are a student-led organization that strives to expose students to STEM topics through robotics. We have a major focus for our members to learn gracious professionalism, engineering skills, logical thinking, problem-solving, and management skills that they will be able to use throughout their life.
