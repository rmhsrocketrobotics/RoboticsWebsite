Rocket Robotics, First Tech Challenge (FTC) Team 22565, is an up and coming student-led open team at Richard Montgomery High School. Based in the Rockville, Maryland area, we strive to expose students to STEM topics through robotics while learning life-long skills

OUTREACH EVENTS

Rocket Robotics pride ourselves in our outreach events based in serving our community and providing accessibility. Check out just a few of our events below! 👇

CODING CLASSES
📍Rockville & Potomac Libraries📍

Rocket Robotics has been hosting six-week Java and Python courses at the Rockville Memorial Library and the Potomac Library since December 2024. Our courses are free of charge and open to all young people aged 8-14 who are curious about coding. If you're interested, reach out and see when we'll be at your library!

ROBOT SHOWCASE
📍Childrens National Hospital📍

On September 23rd of 2025, Rocket Robotics hosted a workshop at Children's National Hospital in Washington D.C. We brought Lego SPIKE Kits to teach basic engineering principles and gave a demo of our robot.

NASA TOUR
📍Goddard Space Flight Center📍

On July 18 of 2025, Rocket Robotics and family were given a tour of NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center by one of our Co-captains who was interning there over the summer. We got the chance to speak with engineers, check out NASA facilities, and even take home pieces of the thermal blanket used during the construction of the James Webb Space Telescope!

FTC MARCH MADNESS
📍Quince Orchard HS📍

On March 22nd of 2025, Rocket Robotics participated in FTC March Madness, an unofficial competition hosted by team 25611 Robotechs. It was a great opportunity to engage with other FTC teams as well as the surrounding community!

NASA ENGINEER PANEL
📍Richard Montgomery HS📍

Rocket Robotics held a panel with 4 engineers from NASA to learn what the daily life of a professional engineer looked like. This even was open to anyone who wanted to join!

ROBOTICS FAIR
📍Snowden Farm ES📍

Rocket Robotics participated in an FTC fair at Snowden Farm ES to introduce STEM, robotics, and FIRST to elementary and middle school students.

STEM NIGHTS
📍Middle/Elementary Schools📍

Rocket Robotics is a frequent presenter at local middle and elementary school STEM nights, and have presented at 5 this year! We use these opportunities to inform students about FIRST and get them engaged with STEM!
